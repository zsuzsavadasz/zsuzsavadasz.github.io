Hungarian Audiobook Library App

This is a side project I started to work on with my partner while improving my skills to design and develop Android applications. We’ve created a mobile app that makes hundreds of Hungarian poems and novels available for free to play on Android mobile, Android TV, Android Auto and Chromecast. The accessibility improvements, visual and functional refinements are still a work in development.

Trade Me homepage redesign

Trade Me is New Zealand’s largest and most influential internet auction and eCommerce platform. I’ve worked on two of the company’s most recent homepage redesigns. As part of a cross-functional team, the first redesign focused on making the homepage more engaging for users and encouraging the discovery of the wide variety of options Trade Me offers. Building on this work the goal of the second redesign has been to make the homepage more intuitive and on-brand applying our new design system.

Trade Me log in and registration

This project looked at improving the registration and log in experiences of Trade Me’s website while also updating their look and feel. Our goal has been to create an intuitive, on-brand user interface, that guide users in a positive and helpful way. I’ve lead the redesign efforts of this work and suggested and designed form validation improvements to help users along their journey.

My Trade Me

My Trade Me is the personal and personalised mission control, where Trade Me’s members go to take action relating to their account, shopping or browsing activities. Our goal has been to create an intuitive on brand My Trade Me that supports members in a delightful and helpful way. I’ve lead the redesign to update the look and feel as well as the navigation and information architecture of this section to provide a framework for future needs.

BeClub mobile app

The BeClub is Beintoo’s standalone loyalty app that rewards users for their engagement with brands when shopping online and in-store. I have collaborated in the app’s development from its inception and was responsible for its continuous development. I introduced new features, and conducted interviews, user tests and usability research to further improve the app.

BeClub website

The BeClub, Beintoo’s loyalty program, allows mobile users to earn loyalty points, by completing sponsored missions within the app. Once signed up, members can redeem their loyalty points to pick up gift cards and other prizes in the BeClub. We created a consumer facing responsive website that helps members better understand what the BeClub can offer them.

BeClub integration in Fruit Ninja

This was my very first independent project as a UX designer. In December 2013, our company was really excited to integrate the BeClub engagement platform in the popular Halfbrick game, Fruit Ninja. As a member of a small dedicated team, I was responsible for the entire user experience of the integration. I worked closely with both Halfbrick’s and Beintoo’s teams to translate business needs into fun new features.

GE Lighting global website

I joined GE Lighting when the company was going through the redesign of its global website and e-catalogue. The project was run to an incredibly tight deadline led by the EMEA HQ and our small team was working around the clock to ensure a successful delivery. I was responsible for building out the new site structure, scouting and editing imagery and ensuring that the pages are in line with the visual and conceptual guidelines.
